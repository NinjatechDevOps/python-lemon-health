Delete Account

Deleting your account will permanently remove your profile and all related data. This action cannot be undone, except where we must retain information for legal or security reasons.

How to delete your account:

  • 1. Open the Profile menu (From the side menu).
  • 2. Tap Delete Account at the bottom of screen.
  • 3. Confirm by selecting Yes.
